SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on 1 January 2025 between ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd and Mr. Ramesh Kumar.

1. Services:
The Service Provider shall deliver software development services as per company requirements.

2. Payment:
The Client shall pay INR 50000 per month for the services.

3. Confidentiality (NDA):
The Service Provider shall keep all company data confidential and shall not disclose it to any third party.

4. Intellectual Property:
All intellectual property created during the project shall belong exclusively to ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd.

5. Non-Compete:
The Service Provider shall not work with any competitor for a period of 2 years after termination of this contract.

6. Termination:
The Client may terminate this agreement at any time without prior notice.

7. Lock-in Period and Auto-Renewal:
This contract shall have a lock-in period of 1 year and shall auto-renew every year unless terminated.

8. Indemnity:
The Service Provider shall indemnify the Client against any damages or losses.

9. Penalty:
A penalty of INR 20000 shall be imposed for breach of contract.

10. Jurisdiction:
Any dispute shall be subject to the jurisdiction of Chennai courts.

11. Ambiguous Clause:
The Service Provider shall perform duties in a reasonable manner as per company expectations.

Signed at Chennai.
